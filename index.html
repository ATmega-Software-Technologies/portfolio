Thinai

Local Offline AI 2026 · Android

On-device LLM · Privacy-first · Play Store

PROBLEM Cloud AI assistants leak your data, need internet, and add server-side latency, which fails private, offline, and low-connectivity users.

Thinai runs state-of-the-art small language models directly on the user's Android device — no cloud, no account, no telemetry. Built on llama.cpp with ARM64-optimised inference and a curated registry of mobile-ready models (Gemma, Phi, Qwen2).

Open on Play Store

Thoogai

தோகை · A Tamil-first AI companion 2026 · Android

Tamil AI · Cultural LLM · Play Store

PROBLEM General-purpose LLMs flatten cultural nuance and underserve Tamil speakers. Their literature, idiom, and philosophical context is rarely treated as a first-class domain.

A Tamil-first AI companion that understands Sangam literature, classical poetry, and the cultural context behind the language. Tuned for bilingual Tamil and English conversation, with life advice grounded in Tamil philosophical traditions.
